The name of the series:Money Heist

The Money Heist series is a crime and thriller series from Spain, and the original name of the series is Lacasa de Papel, which means house of paper. The heads of Netflix have said that the reason for changing the name of the Money Heist series in English is to avoid its resemblance to the House of Cards series. Money Heist was originally slated for a 15-episode run on Antenna TV in 2017, before Netflix acquired the rights in 2019. With the huge investment of the Netflix network, the production of other seasons of this series continued and so far 4 seasons have been released. The complex and very strong script of the Money Heist series and the attractive performances of the actors have distinguished this series from many other series in this category.

The name of the series:Money Heist

The story of the money theft series is about a group of professional thieves with a personal management named Professor. The professor, who is the mastermind of this group, designs a big robbery plan from the National Mint of Spain. He has thought and worked for years to plan this plan accurately and smartly, and now it is time to implement his plan. Each of the thieves in this group has their own reasons for cooperating with the professor's plan. In order not to reveal their identity, the members of this group choose the name of one of the different capitals of the world according to the professor's order. Names like Moscow, Berlin, Tokyo, Nairobi, Denver, Oslo, Rio and Helsinki. Their robbery plan succeeds with many risks and dangers and just at the moment when they think they have failed. During this operation, the professor and his team manage to commit a historical robbery and rob the Spanish National Mint.
